Who We Are

Built on three pillars of care

R1SE stands for Recovery, Intervention, and Support Effort — a nonprofit rooted in the belief that community is the most powerful healing force there is.

We serve individuals and families navigating addiction, mental health challenges, trauma, and life transitions. No judgment. No cost. Just people helping people rise.

🌱

Recovery

Sustainable, compassionate pathways out of addiction and crisis — with peer support, therapy, and community.

🤝

Intervention

Early identification and outreach to connect people with help before situations reach a breaking point.

💜

Support

Ongoing resources, safe spaces, and a community that shows up — because healing is a long-term journey.
