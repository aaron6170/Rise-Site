What We Stand For

One mission, three pillars.

Everything R1SE does is built around these three commitments to our community.

🌿

Recovery

Sustainable, compassionate pathways out of addiction and crisis — with peer support, community, and the resources to move forward.

🤝

Intervention

Early identification and outreach to connect people with help before situations reach a breaking point. Prevention is part of care.

💜

Support

Ongoing resources, safe spaces, and a community that shows up — because healing is a long-term journey, not a single moment.
